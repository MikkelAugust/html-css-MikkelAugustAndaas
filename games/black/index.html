Black

In the haunting world of Black, you face the undead legions that have risen to consume all life. Armed with dark magic and ancient weapons, you’ll descend into cursed lands—from abandoned villages to eerie forests shrouded in mist. Confront terrifying creatures, uncover cryptic runes, and unravel the secrets of those who walk beyond death. Every encounter tests your strength, as you battle to uncover the truth behind the undead plague. With each victory, the shadows grow darker. Will you end the nightmare, or become part of it? Embrace the darkness, for only the strong survive.

  • Genre: Si-fi / Action
  • Released: 2012
  • Age Rating: 16+
  • Price: $49.99
